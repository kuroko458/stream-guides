Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to contact Stream Guides?

No. Anyone can contact us using the contact form. You don't need to create an account.

Can I request a new streaming guide?

Yes. We regularly add new guides based on community feedback. Send us your suggestion using the contact form.

Can I report incorrect information?

Absolutely. If you notice outdated OBS settings, bitrate recommendations or platform changes, please let us know.

Do you provide technical support?

We provide general guidance about OBS, streaming platforms and our website. We cannot offer remote PC troubleshooting.

Can companies contact you?

Yes. Businesses can contact us regarding partnerships, sponsorships and collaborations.

Is my information shared?

No. Information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not sold to third parties.
