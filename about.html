Frequently Asked Questions

Is Stream Guides free to use?

Yes. Every guide, calculator and resource on Stream Guides is completely free.

No. OBS Studio is developed by the OBS Project. Stream Guides is an independent educational website and is not affiliated with OBS Project.

How often is the website updated?

We regularly review our content whenever YouTube, Twitch, Kick, Loco or OBS release important updates or change their streaming recommendations.

Can beginners use these guides?

Absolutely. Our tutorials are written for both beginners and experienced creators with clear explanations and practical recommendations.

Do you recommend paid software?

We recommend both free and premium tools when they genuinely improve the streaming experience. Whenever possible, we also provide free alternatives.

Can I suggest improvements?

Yes. We welcome suggestions for new guides, calculators and features that can help the streaming community.
